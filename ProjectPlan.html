Project Plan: Top 10 NBA Players Site

Purpose & Audience

This site will display my 10 favorite NBA players of all time through an animated, interactive slideshow. Each player links to a dedicated page with highlights, mixtapes, stats, and info. The intended audience is NBA fans who want a fun and engaging way to explore player info and stats.

Design: Colors, Fonts, & Layout

Each player page will use colors and fonts inspired by their main NBA team or personality, maintaining a consistent layout site-wide. The main slideshow and mixtape sections will be flashy and animated to make navigation fun, while the informational sections will be clean and simple to help users focus on the content.

SEO Keywords & Related Sites

Keywords included in the <meta> tag: NBA, basketball, top 10 NBA players, sports stats. Searching these keywords brings up sites like Basketball Reference and Statmuse, which are similar to my planned informational content.

User Experience & Navigation

The site aims to balance flashy, animated slideshows and mixtapes with straightforward, easy-to-read informational pages. Navigation buttons will allow users to move smoothly between pages, ensuring the site is both fun and easy to use.

Additional Features for SEO & Accessibility

To improve viewer experience and SEO, the site will include meta descriptions, alt text for images, and consistent use of semantic HTML elements. These will help make the site more discoverable and accessible to all users.

Plan page created as part of a web development coding project. The final site will include 3-5 pages and a shared CSS file, publicly hosted.
