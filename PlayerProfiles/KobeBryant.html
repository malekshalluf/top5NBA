Career Timeline

1996 - Drafted 13th overall by the Charlotte Hornets and traded to the Lakers

1998 - Youngest NBA All-Star in history at the time

2000-02 - Won three straight NBA Championships with Shaquille O'Neal

2006 - Scored 81 points against the Toronto Raptors

2008 - Won NBA Most Valuable Player (MVP)

2009-10 - Won back-to-back NBA titles, earning Finals MVP both times

2008 & 2012 - Won Olympic gold medals with Team USA

2016 - Retired after dropping 60 points in his final NBA game

2020 - Posthumously inducted into the Naismith Memorial Basketball Hall of Fame
